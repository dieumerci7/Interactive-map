1989
This map shows the percentages of residents of each oblast categorized by ethnicity and native languages on the territory of today’s Ukraine according to the results of the 1979 Soviet census.
Before 1991, the Ukrainian SSR consisted of 25 oblasts and two cities of republican subordination, Kyiv and Sevastopol.
This map reflects the administrative division of Ukraine as of 2015 and should be used only as a reference.

Sources:
Natsional’nyi sostav naselennia SSR po dannym Vsesoiuznoi perepisi naseleniia 1989 g. (Moscow, Finansy i statistika, 1991)
